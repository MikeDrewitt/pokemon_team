Pokemon Team Statistics Application

This application will help show team statistics for all pokemon in party based on types, stats, and other characteristics of the team. It will allow you to change the moveset of each pokemon within their legal moves.

The navbar above will lead you to an index of all availible pokemon should you want to do more research on one before adding it to your team. To add your first pokemon, use the below search field to find it. Once the query returns you'll be able to add it to the team with the + icon.

Some things to note

  • This box will go away once you add your first pokemon.
  • If you refresh/ leave this page your team will not persist, as there is no DB setup.
